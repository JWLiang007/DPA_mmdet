1.Environment

GPU: single NVIDIA GTX 1080Ti
python: 3.6.8
tensorflow-gpu==1.13.1
tensorboard==1.13.1
ModernGL==4.2.2
ModernGL.ext.obj==1.0.0
Pillow==8.0.1
pyrr==0.10.3


2.Running

python3 attack.py

The pretrained model 'faster_rcnn_inception_v2_coco_2017_11_08' will be downloaded automatically.

The generated camouflages will be saved under the directory of  './person/imagedir'.

The tensorboard logs will be saved under the directory of  './person/logdir'.
